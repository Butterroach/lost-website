Lost by Butterroach

Lost is a lightweight hosts manager made for Linux in Python and Qt 6.

You can add hosts, update hosts, and remove hosts all in a nifty and easy-to-use UI.

Lost is also open source, licensed under the GNU General Public License v3 (or, at your choice, any later version).
